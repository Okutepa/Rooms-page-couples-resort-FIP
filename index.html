ROOMS

At our Couples Resort, we’ve designed your stay to feel like a cherished journey into nature’s embrace. Each of our spacious accommodations is a blend of rustic charm and contemporary allure, curated by top designers and enriched by our owner’s collection of fine art. Your Wilderness escape begins with the promise of cozy intimacy and a touch of adventure.

CHATEAUS

Discover Old European Charm with Private Gazebos, Balconies, and Outdoor Hot Tubs, all with Scenic Lake and Forest Views

VILLAS

Our Boutique Spa Villas offer an intimate escape—Outdoor Hot Tub, Whirlpool, Sauna, Steam Room—all curated with Old European Elegance by 'The Art Shoppe.' Your perfect getaway awaits you!

EXOTICS

Embark on adventure with our HOT TUB EXOTIC Jr. SUITES! Indulge in wet delights, spacious exotic vibes,and a touch of Africa.

MASTERS

Immerse in Algonquin Park's magic. Enjoy an Outdoor Hot Tub, a romantic Master's Jr Suite with water indulgences like a Whirlpool, Steam Room, and 4 Shower Heads. Your haven in Muskoka awaits!

CHALETS

Algonquin Rustic Luxury Chalets: Lakefront, dark cedar finish, private hot tubs. Experience Canadian Cabins with indoor Whirlpool and outdoor Hot Tub. Embrace the seasons on the sun deck or winter ice skating

JUNIORS

Indulge yourself in the large double in-room Whirlpool Bath Tub while having a romantic evening by your own crackling wood burning fire. You will sleep well with a luxury king bed by designer Marshall Mattress. Stream Netflix on the 50" Tv in your room.
